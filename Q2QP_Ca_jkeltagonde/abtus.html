WHO WE ARE
The community plays a major role in improving the lives of stray cats. We are a dedicated advocacy group committed to improving the lives of stray cats. Our team comprises passionate individuals, including veterinarians and volunteers, working collaboratively to provide shelter, veterinary care, and community education.



VISION
Creating communities where stray cats are embraced, receiving care and inspiring positive change through collective empathy. Reduce the number of stray cats in the world.



MISSION
To compassionately address the needs of stray cats by offering shelter, veterinary care, and community education. Through innovative initiatives, we strive to create communities where stray cats are embraced, promoting a better life for every feline friend.



HOW TO HELP/PARTICIPATE
Join our cause by volunteering your time and skills at our dedicated shelter, assisting in providing veterinary care, and participating in community education programs. Donate to support our initiatives, or consider adopting a stray cat and spreading awareness about responsible pet ownership. Together, we can make a positive impact on the lives of stray cats and create a more compassionate community for all.
